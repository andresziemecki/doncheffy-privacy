Privacy Policy

Don Cheffy mobile application (iOS & Android)
Last updated: January 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Don Cheffy (“I”, “me”, “my”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the Don Cheffy mobile application (the “App”).

The data controller is an individual developer based in Switzerland.
Contact: support@doncheffy.com

1. Information collected

1.1 Information you provide

  • Email address (used for account creation, authentication via email/password, and support).

1.2 Photos and camera

The App may request access to your device camera to allow you to take pictures. Pictures you choose to upload are stored securely using Firebase Storage. You can control camera permissions at any time in your device settings.

1.3 Analytics and diagnostics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to understand how features are used and Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix technical issues. These services collect technical information related to your device and app usage, such as app interactions and crash data.

2. How information is used

  • To provide and operate the App and its features
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To store and display user-uploaded pictures
  • To improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • To respond to support requests

3. Payments

The App offers paid products and services through:

  • Apple App Store In-App Purchases
  • Google Play In-App Purchases

Payments are processed directly by Apple or Google. I do not receive or store your full payment card details. I may receive purchase and subscription status information required to enable paid features.

4. Data sharing

Personal data is not sold. Data may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate the App (such as Firebase) or when required by law.

5. Data retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the App and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your rights (GDPR & Switzerland)

If you are located in the EU/EEA or UK, you have rights under the GDPR. If you are located in Switzerland, you have rights under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP).

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correction or deletion
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability where applicable
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights or request account deletion, contact: support@doncheffy.com

7. International data processing

Firebase services are provided by Google and may process data on servers outside Switzerland or the EU. Google applies appropriate safeguards as described in its privacy documentation.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and personal data from children is not knowingly collected.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.

10. Contact
